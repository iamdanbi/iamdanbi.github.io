Hello, I am Danbi Kim.

I am a researcher with a background in Mathematics and Aerospace Engineering from KAIST, broadly interested in applied mathematics and quantitative approaches to understanding complex systems. I am particularly drawn to problems where mathematical structure and real-world phenomena intersect. More recently, I have developed a growing interest in statistics and statistical learning, motivated by their role in extracting insight from increasingly complex and high-dimensional data.

For an overview of my academic work and research activities, please see Academics. If you are interested in my personal projects, experiments beyond academia, or simply who I am as a person, I invite you to explore Who am I.
